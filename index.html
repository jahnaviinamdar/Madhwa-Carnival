About Madhwa Carnival

Madhwa Carnival is a thoughtfully curated evening spiritual gathering to welcome the New Year with positivity, unity, and joy.

It is a space for children, youth, and families to come together, perform, share stories, express creativity, and celebrate the richness of our community in a warm and inclusive atmosphere.

Who Can Participate

  • Open to all age groups from 3 to 35 years
  • Children, youth, individuals, and groups are welcome
  • Participants may perform, volunteer, or host stalls
  • No prior experience required — enthusiasm matters most

Event Flow

  • Inauguration Ceremony
  • Open Mic Cultural Performances
  • Music, Dance, Drama & Storytelling
  • Creative, Cultural & Food Stalls
  • Community Interaction & Celebration
