About Madhwa Carnival

Madhwa Carnival is a New Year spiritual and cultural gathering created to bring together children, youth, and families from the Madhwa community in an atmosphere of positivity, devotion, creativity, and togetherness.

Who Can Participate

  • Open to all age groups from 3 to 35 years
  • Individuals, groups, and families welcome
  • Open mic cultural programs and stalls
  • No prior experience required

Event Flow

  • Inauguration Ceremony
  • Open Mic Cultural Performances
  • Music, Dance, Drama & Storytelling
  • Creative, Cultural & Food Stalls
  • Community Celebration
