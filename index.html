About Madhwa Carnival

Madhwa Carnival is a New Year spiritual evening celebration created to reconnect, express creativity, and celebrate community through culture, art, and joy.

Who Can Participate

  • Age group: 3 to 35 years
  • Open to individuals and groups
  • Perform, volunteer, or host stalls
  • No experience required

Event Flow

  • Inauguration Ceremony
  • Open Mic Cultural Performances
  • Music, Dance, Drama & Storytelling
  • Creative & Cultural Stalls
