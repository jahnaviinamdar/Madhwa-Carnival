About Madhwa Carnival

Madhwa Carnival is a thoughtfully curated New Year spiritual gathering designed to bring families, children, and youth together in a warm, inclusive, and festive atmosphere.

It is an evening celebration focused on cultural expression, creativity, devotion, and community bonding.
