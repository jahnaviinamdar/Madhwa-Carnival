About

Madhwa Carnival is a New Year spiritual evening celebration where children, youth, and families come together to perform, create, interact, and celebrate community bonding through culture and joy.

Who Can Participate

  • Age group: 3 to 35 years
  • Individuals & groups welcome
  • Performers, stall owners & volunteers
  • No prior experience required

Event Flow

  • Inauguration Ceremony
  • Open Mic Cultural Performances
  • Music, Dance, Drama & Storytelling
  • Creative & Cultural Stalls
  • Community Interaction & Celebration
