About the Carnival

Madhwa Carnival is a vibrant evening spiritual gathering designed to celebrate talent, expression, and unity within the Madhwa community. This New Year’s Carnival is a space to reconnect, showcase creativity, build friendships, and celebrate shared values in a joyful, welcoming environment.

Who Can Participate

  • Open to all age groups from 3 to 35 years
  • Individuals, families, and groups are welcome
  • Participants can perform, volunteer, or host stalls
  • No prior experience required — passion and enthusiasm matter

Event Flow

  • Inauguration Ceremony
  • Open Mic Cultural Performances
  • Music, Dance, Drama & Storytelling
  • Creative & Cultural Stalls
  • Community Interaction & Celebration
