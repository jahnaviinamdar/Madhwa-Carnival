About Madhwa Carnival

Madhwa Carnival is a thoughtfully curated New Year spiritual carnival designed to unite the Madhwa community in an atmosphere of positivity, joy, and shared celebration.

Who Can Participate

  • Open to all age groups from 3 to 35 years
  • Children, youth, individuals & groups welcome
  • Open mic cultural performances & stalls
  • No prior experience required

Event Flow

  • Inauguration Ceremony
  • Open Mic Cultural Programs
  • Music, Dance, Drama & Storytelling
  • Creative, Cultural & Food Stalls
  • Community Celebration
